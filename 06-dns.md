_Exam Tips_:

- ELBs do not have pre-defined IPv4 addresses; you resolve to them using a DNS
- Know the difference between an Alias Record and a CNAME
- Given the choice, always choose an Alias Record over a CNAME 

- Common DNS Types:
  - SOA Records
  - NS Records
  - A Records
  - CNAMES
  - MX Records 
  - PTR Records
